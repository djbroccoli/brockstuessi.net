ECHOES OF SEEMA

Echoes of Seema if a digital exhibit showcasing fragments of the Seema Weatherwax interview collection held in the specil collection at UC Santa Cruz. I processed the collection and created the exhibit as a CART Fellow at UCSC in the Fall of 2020.

UNDERGROUND ARCHIVE PROJECT

THe Underground Archive Project is an ongoing effort to digitally archive music director notes written on the covers of records in the WNUR-FM collection. I started the project with an undergraduate research grant at Northwestern and continue to maintain the site and digital collection.
